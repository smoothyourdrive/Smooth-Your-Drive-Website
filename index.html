General Servicing

A regular car service checks the condition of your car. It can assess everything from general wear and tear to fluids, brakes, the engine, and more. Our car service involves up to 50 or more components, systems checks and adjustments including:

  • An engine oil change and oil filter replacement.
  • Checking lights, tyres, exhaust and operations of brakes and steering.
  • Ensuring your engine is ‘tuned’ to run in its peak condition.
  • Checking hydraulic fluid and coolant levels.
  • Checking the cooling system (from radiators in your car to pumps and hoses).
  • Suspension checks
  • Steering alignment
  • Testing the car’s battery condition

Scanning and Coding

A car scanning is done by a electronic tool used to interface with,the Vehicle control module and sometimes to reprogram. But it’s not a silver bullet that will always tell you exactly what’s wrong. A scanner gives you real-time “live” information so you see the same data your car’s computer is seeing. That saves you the hassle of diving under the hood withpout piercing wires and taking sensor readings.

A full Vehicle restoration entails completely disassembling the vehicle and restoring each and every part and system. Restoring a car requires an intimate understanding of the mechanical aspects of the vehicle, an eye for cosmetic details, and an appreciation for the work on the car. Not only are our team members experts in their fields, but they have a passion for cars and car restoration. Each project we accept matters to us, and we work closely with our customers to ensure their vision can come to life through the restoration. This process limits ongoing maintenance and future restoration situations.

Engine Overhauling

An engine overhauling is a complete rebuild of the engine. The Crankshaft rod/main parts are polished and rods are resized if necessary. Cam is checked polished or replaced. Bore is checked, resized if necessary, honed if not. Deck is checked, flattened if required. Head is cleaned, valves are re-seated, valve springs checked and replaced, lifters are replaced. Other odds and ends of overhead valve will be checked and replaced according to the condition. Engine is reassembled with new bearings, rings, gaskets, timing belt or chain, oil pump, often water pump. Professionally reassembled in such a way that the customer should get another lifetime out of it.

Dent and Paint

Denting and painting process is very tedious and heavy labour work. The Denting process removes visible deformities on the exterior body of the car. In case of accidents, collision of vehicle denting and painting of the parts are preferred as it helps save the additional cost of the new part used.
